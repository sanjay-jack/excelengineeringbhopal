Excel Engineering

Established in 2007, Excel Engineering is a fully integrated manufacturer of hydro-mechanical equipment. Dedicated to providing high-quality, engineered solutions tailored to client needs and expectations, Excel Engineering operates from its Bhopal facility. The company is among a select group that serves esteemed clients throughout the entire project lifecycle, offering design, drawing, fabrication, supply, erection, and commissioning for all its products.

About Excel Engineering

Leading Turn-Key Solutions for Design, Manufacturing, and Project Execution

Excel Engineering is a design, manufacturing, and project execution company with a strong management focus on turnkey solutions. Serving the power and oil & gas industries, Excel Engineering was incorporated in 2007 with the objective of engaging in fabrication, erection, and commissioning work for power plants.

Services & Solutions

We are extensively involved in providing services to clients across various industries. Our experienced teams deliver high-quality services in sandblasting and epoxy painting, hydro-mechanical powerhouse maintenance, and annual maintenance contracts for dam gates. Additionally, we specialize in the design and manufacture of a wide range of products.
