Projects

I take great pride in introducing you to one of the pioneers in the engineering industry, Excel Engineering, located at 30/5 Sector-H, Industries Area, Govindpura, Bhopal, M.P. Our company has been in the engineering business for over 15 years and continues to grow.

Excel Engineering specializes in design, manufacturing, and project execution, with a strong management focus on turnkey solutions. We provide design, drawing, fabrication, supply, and erection services for radial gates, stoplog gates, vertical spillway gates, and all types of electrically and manually operated rope drum hoists and monorails. Additionally, we offer automatic lifting beams, gantry cranes, hydraulic hoists, vent liners, sluice gates, and stop dam gates.
